# Expression of Interest for Data Scientist Position (EIC Transition Project)

## Role Overview
We are building an agentic data analytics platform powered by ML models and autonomous agents. We are looking for a Data Scientist to create benchmark datasets, design experiments, and generate actionable insights to drive both product and model development.

This role is highly cross-functional: you will work with AI architects, ML engineers, and product teams to ensure the system is grounded in realistic, measurable use cases.

## Key Responsibilities
- Build and curate benchmark datasets for analytics, ML, and agent evaluation
- Design, run, and analyze experiments and competitions to validate model/system performance
- Generate realistic use cases and scenarios for agentic workflows
- Work with the product team to conduct pilot studies and A/B tests to inform design and adoption
- Perform data analysis, feature engineering, and statistical modeling
- Collaborate with ML Integration Engineer and AI Architect to evaluate models and agent behavior
- Automate data pipelines and analysis scripts for reproducible results
- Communicate findings clearly to both technical and non-technical stakeholders

## Required Skills
- Master’s degree (or higher) in Computer Science, Mathematics, Statistics, or related quantitative field 
- Strong Python proficiency (pandas, NumPy, scikit-learn, Jupyter)
- Solid understanding of machine learning algorithms and evaluation metrics
- Experience with data wrangling, preprocessing, feature engineering, and dataset management
- Knowledge of experimental design and pilot studies
- Ability to generate realistic synthetic or curated datasets
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
- Ability to communicate complex results clearly and visually

## Nice to Have
- Experience with ML benchmarking or competitions (Kaggle-style or internal evaluation setups)
- Familiarity with data visualization libraries (Matplotlib, Seaborn, Plotly)
- Experience working with large-scale datasets
- Knowledge of AI/agent evaluation metrics (RAG, reasoning chains, multi-step workflows)
- Familiarity with agile workflows and CI/CD pipelines for data projects

## What Success Looks Like
- High-quality benchmark datasets aligned with agentic analytics needs
- Rigorous, reproducible data analyses and model evaluations
- Realistic use cases and pilot studies feeding directly into product decisions
- Clear communication of findings to product, ML, Marketing, and agent teams
- Contribution to continuous improvement of models and agent performance
