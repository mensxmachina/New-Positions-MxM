8.	Expression of Interest for ML Researcher Position (EIC Transition Project)
Role Overview
We are building a state-of-the-art agentic data analytics platform. We are seeking a Machine Learning Researcher with a strong foundation in probabilistic modeling, causality, and advanced ML to drive innovative algorithms, benchmark studies, and methodological research.
This role is research-intensive but highly applied: your work will inform the ML engine, agent reasoning, and analytical capabilities of the platform.
Key Responsibilities
•	Conduct research in Graph-based models (Bayesian networks, correlation networks, and causal inference )
•	Design, implement, and evaluate ML algorithms suitable for agentic analytics systems 
•	Collaborate with Data Scientists and ML Integration Engineers to implement research insights in production 
•	Contribute to research publications, conferences, or white papers 
•	Participate in collaborative projects (internal and external) and drive knowledge transfer 
•	Develop and benchmark new methods against existing algorithms on curated datasets 
•	Mentor junior team members on advanced ML concepts and best practices 
•	Stay current on emerging ML research and translate relevant methods into actionable applications 
Required Skills
•	Master’s degree (or higher) in Computer Science, Mathematics, Statistics, or related quantitative field 
•	Strong background in Bayesian modeling, correlation networks, and causal inference 
•	Hands-on Python programming and familiarity with ML frameworks (PyTorch, TensorFlow, scikit-learn) 
•	Experience designing and analyzing experiments to validate ML methods 
•	Track record of research publication or participation in research projects 
•	Ability to translate complex theoretical concepts into applied solutions 
Nice to Have
•	PhD in a relevant field 
•	Experience with probabilistic programming frameworks (Pyro, Edward, Stan) 
•	Knowledge of agent systems, reasoning under uncertainty, or multi-step planning algorithms 
•	Familiarity with real-world datasets and applied ML in analytics 
•	Experience in ML benchmarking or competition frameworks
What Success Looks Like
•	Publication-quality research with direct application to your platform
•	Proven improvements in model performance through advanced causal and probabilistic methods
•	Research insights successfully translated into production ML pipelines
•	Benchmarks and methods that guide agent reasoning and decision-making
•	Establishment of the platform as a technically credible, research-backed system
