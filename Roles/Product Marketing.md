# Expression of Interest for Product Marketing Position (EIC Transition Project)

## Role Overview
• We are building an agentic data analytics platform powered by AI agents and machine learning. We are looking for a Product Marketing Manager to define how the product is positioned, communicated, and adopted in the market.
• This role sits at the intersection of product, engineering, and growth, translating complex technical capabilities into clear value propositions and driving user acquisition through targeted marketing strategies.

## Key Responsibilities
- Define and own product positioning, messaging and dissemination strategies
- Translate technical features (AI agents, ML pipelines, analytics) into clear user value
- Lead the creation of a community of users and contributors 
- Assist in developing and executing go-to-market (GTM) strategies
- Create high-quality content and narratives:
  - Website copy
  - Product pages
  - Case studies
  - Technical explainers
- Own and grow social media presence (especially LinkedIn, Twitter/X)
- Plan and execute product launches and feature announcements
- Collaborate with Product Manager and AI Architect to ensure message accuracy
- Run experiments on growth channels (organic + paid)
- Track and optimize conversion funnels and engagement metrics

## Required Skills
- Proven experience (3+ years) in product marketing, ideally in SaaS or tech products
- Strong ability to simplify complex technical concepts
- Experience managing social media strategy and content
- Excellent writing and storytelling skills
- Understanding of product-led growth (PLG) principles
- Ability to work closely with technical teams and understand product capabilities

## Nice to Have
- Experience marketing AI/ML, data platforms, or developer tools
- Familiarity with agent systems, LLMs, or analytics tools
- Experience with content marketing, SEO, and behavioral analytics tools
- Hands-on experience with analytics tools (Google Analytics, Mixpanel, etc.)
- Basic understanding of design tools (Figma, Canva)

## What Success Looks Like
- Clear, differentiated product positioning in a crowded AI market
- Strong, consistent brand voice across all channels
- Growing inbound interest and qualified leads
- High engagement on social channels (especially technical audiences)
- Effective product launches that drive adoption
